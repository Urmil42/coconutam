About us

Turning Waste into Wonder

At COCOnutam, we see potential where others see waste. Every year, millions of coconut shells and husks are discarded—but we believe they deserve a second life. That’s why we transform these natural byproducts into beautiful, sustainable products that are kind to both people and the planet.

Our Story

What started as a simple idea—to reduce wasteand promote sustainability—has grown into a movement. We are a team ofeco-conscious creators, artisans, and innovators dedicated to craftinghigh-quality, upcycled coconut products. By blending traditional craftsmanshipwith modern design, we bring you eco-friendly alternatives that make adifference.
